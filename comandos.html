Controls of the Game - Medieval Quest

Controls List:

  • Run to the Right: Press Button 'D'
  • Run to the Left: Press Button 'A'
  • Jump: Press Button 'W'
  • Attack: Press Button 'Space'
  • Use Shield: Press Button 'Ctrl'

Game Objective:

Game Objective is very simple! You as a strong and smart Medieval Fighter have to defeat all the monsters that appeared in front of you.

This first monsters consists on skeletons and minotaurs.

They are the easy part of your Quest!!

Next and only after you defeat all of this first enimies you unluck the BOSS FIGHT!!

This BOSS is the most dreaded and fierce of all kingdom and all the fighters of our land FEAR HIM.

You are the only hope of our lands to get rid of this fearless monster!!
